About this place

Purpose

I used to collect gadgets(like small glass bolls) into a glass jar. This site is essentially equivlent to the glass jar, but with far more volume and capability to keep ideas. I would like to share my thoughts and perhaps discovers here, tease apart knowledge I've learned, and also interpret and demonstrate my previous projects/works.

This site consists of 4 parts, including a mini StackOverflow, an Album, my tech Blog and a list of projects(yet not been deployed)

mini StackOverflow

A simplified stack overflow version, you can post questions and attach answers. Here are the details:

  • Will handle multiple users, but you need to use google account to login.
  • Create/modify question and answer.
  • Each answer has a vote attribute, you can either up or down vote an answer once.
  • Support tag system, you can search question by tags

Album

A gallery system which allows every user post and delete photos.

  • Handle multiple users, but login in first.
  • Two layers structure, you need firstly create a category before you upload your pictures
  • Category name is required, but before you click your newly generated category, this category won be remembered.
  • Please upload photos via
    • giveing a note(optional)
    • browse and choose your file
    • upload
  • Key words: Blobstore(file size uplimit: 35MB), colorBox

Blog

A tech bolg this is used to store and manage my thoughts and ideas.

  • I introduced ADMIN login from app engine, so basicly speak, only admin has the right to add/delete/modiy articles.
  • But you can still browse it
  • Tag system as usual
  • Rich text editor
  • Sider bar with category list and archive
  • Key words: CKEditor, Admin Login
